About Us

About Ankuram

Ankuram is a website which provides required information about BCA with necessary materials such as notes, syllabus, and question bank for the users/students of BCA field.

Students can ask question problem related to the subject in the BCA forum. People interested in BCA field can take entrance preparation test from website which can boost their confidence for the future entrance examination.
